About the Wine

A wine born from dreams, made with 97% Chardonnay grapes. The aging in small French oak barrels gives it a remarkable complexity and structure. A wine that has evolved over the years, from its name to its closure, always maintaining its character and quality. The screw cap is a recent choice made to ensure the wine's quality is preserved over its long life.

History and Trivia

Dedicated to U2's "The Joshua Tree" album, the wine was first made in 1987 as "Where the Dreams have no end...". The label and name have changed multiple times, including a period as "Were Dreams, now it is just wine!" with a Comet Hale-Bopp on the capsule. The "dot-dot-dot" in the name gives room for personal interpretation, with the winery's being "Where Dreams can happen." The original "Where the Dreams have no end..." label was famously featured in the 1998 Disney film "The Parent Trap."
