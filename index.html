Discover the Menu

Jermann

Italy's Premier White Wine Producer

Since 1881, the Jermann family has been crafting exceptional wines in Friuli-Venezia Giulia, Italy's northeastern region known for its distinctive white wines.

Experience the authentic flavors of Friuli through perfect pairings crafted by Culinary Director Riccardo Longo, featuring dishes that highlight the region's unique culinary heritage.

The Menu

Curated by Culinary Director Riccardo Longo

APERITIVO

TAGLIERE FRIULANO

Prosciutto San Daniele, local Montasio cheese, & frico friulian potato/cheese pancake, fig jam & chestnut honey

Wine Pairing
Pinot Grigio, Jermann
Pinot Grigio made by Italy's premier white wine maker. Beautifully structured notes of citrus, apple, white peach & mineral

ASSAGGI

GNOCCHI DE' SUSINI

Jumbo potato and Grana Padano gnocchi stuffed with fresh plums and topped with brown butter and a touch of cinnamon

Wine Pairing
Ramat Pinot Grigio, Jermann
Refined single vineyard expression of skin contact Pinot Grigio. Rich notes of dried orchard fruit, wild flowers and a distinct mineral edge

PRIMO

CIALSONS

Iconic hand rolled ravioli of Friuli stuffed with a mix of smoked ricotta, potato, pancetta & amaretti. Topped with Grana Padano-butter

Wine Pairing
Dreams, Jermann
Arguably the finest Chardonnay produced in Italy. Complex and elegant notes of creme brulee, ripe exotic fruit, refined citrus, stone fruit, layered pastry & poetic minerality

SECONDO

GULASCH AL RED ANGEL

A dish that demonstrates the Austria-Hungary influence on Friuli's cuisine. Slow braised beef stew with onions, tomato, paprika & garlic simmered with Jermann Pinot Noir & served with polenta

Wine Pairing
Pinot Noir Red Angel, Jermann
Well-orchestrated, slightly herbaceous, with notes of frutti di bosco & spice. Displays elegance, finesse, with excellent balance

DOLCE

STRUDEL AL JERMANN DREAMS

Apple strudel gelato by Italian gelato champion Stefano Biasini served over a Jermann Dreams Chardonnay infused deconstructed warm apple strudel, accents of raisins, cinnamon & walnuts

We thank our guests for joining us on this incredible sensory and culinary journey through the extraordinary flavors of Friuli-Venezia Giulia. Your presence made this evening unforgettable.
