About the Wine

Red Angel is a 100% Pinot Nero (Pinot Noir) named for Silvio Jermann's oldest son, Angelo. While Jermann is known primarily for their white wines, they also deftly craft wines from Pinot Noir. This wine showcases the elegance and finesse of the variety in the Friuli region.

Vintage Notes 2021

A rainy spring gave way to a favorable and warm June and July. August was abnormally hot, which necessitated some irrigation, but the end of the season benefited from significant diurnal shifts which kept the acidity high. Harvest concluded on September 15.

Vinification

Fermentation: Inoculated in stainless steel. Filtered and fined (no animal byproducts used).

Ideal Food Pairings

This wine is best paired with Central European dishes and oven-baked fish. Its delicacy is particularly appreciated with white truffle dishes. It can also be matched with grilled fish, served at 13°C.

Winery History

Jermann Winery was founded in 1881. Silvio Jermann is one of the most influential winemakers in Italy, turning his family's modest farm into a hallmark winery in Friuli. The winery is located at 45°59'19"N 13°28'09"E.

Organoleptic Profile

Tasting Notes

Visual

A fine, brilliant ruby red color typical of Pinot Nero.

Olfactory

Elegant aromas of red berries, with subtle herbal and earthy notes characteristic of the variety and terroir.

Gustatory

Delicate and elegant on the palate with fine tannins and balanced acidity. The wine shows good structure with a persistent, fresh finish.
