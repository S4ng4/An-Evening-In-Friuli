Vintage Notes 2023

Winter was mild with above-average temperatures, allowing pruning to finish by mid-March. Spring saw sprouting from March 30 to April 15, but some varieties were delayed. Flowering was early and concentrated by May 20. Rain fell from early May to mid-June. Summer had dry spells from mid-July to the end of August, along with two hailstorms. Harvest began on August 16 with Pinot Grigio.
